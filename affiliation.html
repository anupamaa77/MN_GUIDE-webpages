Affiliations

In context of Nepal, most of the private or public colleges that provides these various Bachelors courses are
under national affiliation providing universities but some other colleges might be under an international
universities too. Here’s brief information on the major universities that provides management courses across
Nepal.

National Affiliations

Tribhuvan University(TU)

Bachelors of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelors of Business Administration in Finance (BBA-F) etc. are
some among the various types of programs it provides.

Some of the colleges that follow it’s course guidance and provides management courses are:

  • Global College of Management (BBS)
  • Thames International College (BBA, BIM, BBS, BBM)
  • Kathmandu Model College (BBA, BBS, BBM)
  • Prime College (BBA, BIM, BBS, BBM)
  • Saraswati Multiple College (BBS, BBM, BBA, BHM)

Kathmandu University(KU)

Kathmandu University (KU) is a public autonomous university which is also the third oldest university
of Nepal.

Some of the colleges that follow it’s course guidance and provides management courses are:

  • Kathmandu University School Of Management (BBA, BBIS, BHTM)
  • National College of Management (BBA, BHM)
  • Little Angel’s College of Management (BBA, BBIS, BHTM)

Pokahara University(PoU)

The fifth university in Nepal, Pokhara University (PU or PoU), was founded in 1997.

Some of the colleges that follow it’s course guidance and provides management courses are:

  • Ace Institute of Management (BBA)
  • SAIM College (BBA)
  • Quest International College (BBA, BHM)
  • Uniglobe College (BBA)

Purbanchal university(PU)

Purbanchal University offers courses in 74 different subjects through its five main campuses as well
as 123 additional affiliated institutions.

Some of the institutes under it’s management courses guidance are:

  • Don Bosco College (BBA)
  • Kantipur International College (BHM)
  • Silkroad International College (BBA)

International affiliations

Apart from the colleges under above universities there are some college and institutes affiliated to foreign
universities. According to The Government of Nepal, Ministry of Education some of the colleges under
foreign institutes are :

  • Asian Institute of Technology and Management(IMI University center) provides unique course such as - Bachelor degree in International
    Hotel and Tourism Management
  • Birat College for Professional Studies Pvt. ltd.(Asia pacific university of Technology and innovation) located in Biratnagar has BA(Hons) in
    Business Management
  • C.G. Institute of Management Pvt. ltd is located in Minbhawan, which is affiliated to .- Linkokwing University of Creative Technology,
    provides BBA of 4 years.
  • Himalayan college of Management, Pvt. Ltd is under Infrastructure University of Malaysia which also provides BBA.
