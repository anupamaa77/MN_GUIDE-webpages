Management

Management, often known as managing, is the process of administering
organizations, be they government agencies, nonprofit organizations, or
businesses, using the commercial, nonprofit, or political science subfields
of public administration, respectively. In colleges and universities,
management is taught in a variety of subject areas. Public administration,
business administration, and management are three prominent major
degree programs in management. As an academic field, social science
studies management, looking into topics like organizational leadership,
organizational adaptation, and social organization.
